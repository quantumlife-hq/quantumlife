Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Our Privacy Philosophy

QuantumLife is built with privacy at its core. We believe your personal data belongs to you, not us. That's why we designed QuantumLife to run locally on your device whenever possible, with end-to-end encryption for any data that needs to be synced.

Key Privacy Principles:

  • ✓ Local-First: Your data stays on your device by default
  • ✓ Post-Quantum Encryption: Future-proof security for all synced data
  • ✓ You Own Your Data: Export or delete anytime
  • ✓ Open Source: Verify our claims yourself

Information We Collect

Waitlist Information

When you join our waitlist, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Signup timestamp
  • Referral source (if applicable)

When Using QuantumLife

QuantumLife processes various types of personal information to function as your digital twin:

  • Email content and metadata (stored locally)
  • Calendar events (stored locally)
  • Behavioral patterns and preferences (stored locally)
  • Financial data if connected (stored locally with encryption)

Important: This data is processed and stored locally on your device. We do not have access to it.

How We Use Information

  • • Waitlist emails: To notify you about launch and product updates
  • • Account data: To provide and improve the service
  • • Usage analytics: Anonymized, aggregated data to improve the product

Data Storage & Security

We employ industry-leading security practices:

  • Local-first architecture - data stays on your device
  • End-to-end encryption for any synced data
  • Post-quantum cryptographic algorithms (future-proof)
  • Zero-knowledge architecture where possible
  • Regular security audits

Third-Party Services

QuantumLife may integrate with third-party services you choose to connect:

  • Google (Gmail, Calendar) - OAuth authentication only
  • Microsoft (Outlook, Calendar) - OAuth authentication only
  • Financial services (if opted in)

We only request the minimum permissions necessary and never store your third-party credentials.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Export all your data in standard formats
  • Delete your data and account
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request corrections to your data

privacy@quantumlife.ai

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
